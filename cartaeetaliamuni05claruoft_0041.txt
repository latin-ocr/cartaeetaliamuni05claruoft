Endorsed—‘ Confirmaoio Edwardi quarti. Anno 5to.'

In a much later hand—⁴ This Charter granted by King Edward the fourth in
the fist year of his reign whereby the liberties of Kerdiffe are confirmed.'

This Charter is well preserved, and written in a bold. clear hand, with excellent
black ink. The seal is gone. What remains of the plait shews it to have been

composed of blue, red, and white silk, with a single thread of silver wire.

A copy is extant in EM„ Add. MS. 15,662‚ fol. 134.

See also reduced photograph of the original in the ‘Cardiff Records‚' Vol. L, p. 42.

some years ago. There are none now at Cardiff Castle.
